# GRESB Submission Preparation Workflow

## Steps

1. **Data inventory** — audit available energy, water, waste, and certification data by asset
2. **Coverage assessment** — calculate Like-for-Like coverage % and identify gaps
3. **Score estimate** — estimate Management and Performance component scores
4. **Gap analysis** — identify top 5 improvements by points-per-effort
5. **Report draft** — draft key narrative sections (governance, targets, performance)

Pause after each step for review before proceeding.
